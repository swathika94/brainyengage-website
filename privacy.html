Privacy Policy

12. Introduction

Brainy Engage ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use the Brainy Engage app.

By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

13. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information: Name, email address, gender, account type (Instructor / Student / Parent), and password (encrypted).
  • Instructor contact number: Instructors are required to provide a phone number as part of their class listing, so that Students or Parents/Guardians can contact them directly regarding a class. This phone number is visible to Students and Parents/Guardians viewing the listing.
  • Class photos: Instructors are required to upload between 1 and 5 photos when creating a class listing. These photos are visible to Students and Parents/Guardians browsing class listings.
  • Profile information: Profile photo, bio, and instructor qualifications where provided.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns within the App.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Email communications: We send email notifications to the account holder (adult Student or Parent/Guardian) regarding booking confirmations, cancellations, and service updates. There is no in-app messaging system, and instructors do not have access to send messages to students or parents/guardians directly.
  • Booking data: Class bookings, cancellations, and attendance records.

14. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Facilitate class bookings and scheduling.
  • Improve and personalise your experience in the App.
  • Send important service notifications by email (e.g. booking confirmations, cancellations, policy updates).
  • Ensure the safety and security of the platform.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your personal data for targeted advertising.

15. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Instructors' public profiles and class listings — including class photos and the instructor's phone number, which instructors provide so Students or Parents/Guardians can contact them about a class — are visible to Students and Parents/Guardians. Instructors can see the number of students booked and cancellations for their own class listings, but do not have access to the names, email addresses, or any other personal information of individual Students, Parents, or Guardians. We do not collect or display any child's name or other identifying information about a child within the App.
  • Service providers: We may share data with trusted third-party providers (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, data may be transferred to the new entity, with users notified in advance.

16. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see our Account Deletion page for instructions. Upon a verified deletion request, we do not retain your personal account data. Where your account is an Instructor account linked to a class but is not the Class Admin for that class, the class listing and its photos may remain, as they are not solely owned by your account.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

18. Children's Privacy

Brainy Engage is a platform that connects instructors with students for educational classes and sessions. Some classes are attended by adult Students (18+) who hold their own account; others are attended by children, whose classes are booked and managed by a parent or legal guardian who is 18 years of age or older. The App itself is intended to be used and administered only by adults — we do not knowingly collect any personal information directly from children.

All account information — including name, email address, gender, and password — belongs to and is provided solely by the parent or guardian who creates and manages the account. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information about the child attending a class, including but not limited to the child's name, age, date of birth, or any other identifying information.

If we become aware that a child under 18 has created their own account directly, we will promptly suspend the account and delete the associated data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at hello@brainyengage.com and we will take steps to review and, if necessary, delete such information.
