Before you begin

Deleting your account is permanent. This will remove your name, email address, gender, account credentials, and booking history from Brainy Engage. This applies to all account holders, including Parents/Guardians and adult Students.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to hello@brainyengage.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include the full name and email address on your account so we can locate it.
  4. We will confirm your identity and process your request within a reasonable timeframe.
Email Us to Delete My Account

What Gets Deleted

Once your request is verified, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your gender (if provided)
  • Your phone number (if you are an Instructor)
  • Your account credentials
  • Your booking and class history

If you are the Class Admin for a class (the account that created the class listing), deleting your account will also remove that class listing and its associated photos. If you are an Instructor account linked to a class but not the Class Admin, deleting your account removes your personal account data as listed above, but the class listing and its photos remain, since the class is not solely owned by your account.

Data Retention

Upon a verified deletion request, we do not retain your personal account data (name, email, gender, phone number, credentials). Class listings and photos may be retained where your account was not the Class Admin for that class, as described above. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Timeframe

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days of verification. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and associated data have been deleted.

Questions?

Reach us anytime at hello@brainyengage.com, or review our full Privacy Policy.
